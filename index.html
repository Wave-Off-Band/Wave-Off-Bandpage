About

You find yourself on one of those late summer evenings, the sun sinking below the horizon and painting the sky in shades of orange and violet. You soak in its warmth, knowing there aren't many of these evenings left before autumn sets in. That's exactly the vibe Mannheim-based indie punk band WAVE OFF captures with their catchy yet raw sound.

The band's influences span a wide range — from 2000s indie and surf punk to garage rock and post-hardcore. They bring these influences together in their own unique way, creating a sound that feels both nostalgic and refreshingly modern.
